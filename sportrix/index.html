About Us

Kalyani Government Engineering College was established by Govt. of West Bengal in the year 1995. KGEC is an autonomous institute affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam University of Technology (MAKAUT) formally West Bengal University of Technology (WBUT). We have a dedicated team which includes faculty and students for the promotion of sports. Sports were always one of the areas where the students of KGEC always present their power. We have won number of tournaments and participated in various sports events organized by different institutions throughout India. In near future we hope our students will participate and show their best performance in upcoming tournaments. We are starting a fresh cell named ‘SPORTRIX’ for this purpose.
